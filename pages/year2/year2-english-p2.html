Section A



Main Message/Aim

  • Usually the biggest sentence found in the visual text, or the title
  • Summarises the main message of the visual text (read the visual text first)
  • Usually contains an action or informative fact (not something vague and obscure such as "wanted trendsetters" or "aspiring people")


Target Audience

  • Either mentioned or hinted in the poster (if the target audience is hinted, it's easy to miss it out, make sure to really dive within the visual text)
